# TechCorp International Travel Policy
**Version:** 1.3 | **Effective Date:** January 1, 2024 | **Owner:** Finance & Operations

---

## 1. Scope

This policy applies to all international travel. It supplements the main Travel Policy (`policy.md`) with enhanced limits and special rules for overseas trips.

---

## 2. International Per-Diem Limits

Refer to **per_diem_limits.csv** — rows with `type = international`.

All foreign currency amounts are reimbursed in **INR** at the **RBI reference rate** on the date of expense.

---

## 3. Enhanced Approval Requirements

| Amount (INR equivalent) | Approver |
|---|---|
| Up to ₹50,000 | Manager |
| ₹50,001 – ₹2,00,000 | Finance Team |
| Above ₹2,00,000 | CFO |

---

## 4. Flight Rules (International)

- Economy class: All routes
- Business class allowed:
  - Flight duration > 6 hours (with manager approval)
  - Medical documentation

---

## 5. Hotel Rules (International)

- Maximum 5-star rated hotels
- Director approval required for 5-star bookings
- Airbnb allowed in cities without listed hotels (prior HR approval needed)

---

## 6. Forex & Currency

- All expenses in foreign currency must have forex receipts
- Forex receipts required for amounts > $50 / €45 / £40
- Currency conversion: RBI reference rate on date of expense
- Cash advances for forex available via Finance team

---

## 7. Visa & Travel Documents

- Visa fees: Fully reimbursed (actual cost) with receipt
- Travel insurance: Company-approved plans only
- Airport tax / departure tax: Fully reimbursed

---

## 8. Communication Expenses

- Business roaming charges: Reimbursed with itemized bill
- International SIM card: Reimbursed up to ₹500/day
- Personal calls: Not reimbursed
