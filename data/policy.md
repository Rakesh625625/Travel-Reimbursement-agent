# TechCorp Travel Reimbursement Policy
**Version:** 2.1 | **Effective Date:** January 1, 2024 | **Owner:** Finance & Operations

---

## 1. Purpose & Scope

This policy governs all employee travel and expense reimbursements at TechCorp. It applies to all full-time employees, contractors on company-sponsored travel, and consultants traveling on behalf of TechCorp.

All claims must be submitted within **30 days** of the trip end date. Late submissions require manager approval.

---

## 2. Eligible Expense Categories

The following expense types are eligible for reimbursement:

| Category | Code | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Hotel / Accommodation | `hotel` | Within per-diem limits. Max 4-star domestic, 5-star international with Director approval |
| Meals | `meal` | Within per-diem limits. Business meals with clients require guest list |
| Taxi / Cab | `taxi` | Airport transfers, client visits. No luxury vehicles |
| Flight | `flight` | Economy class only. Business class allowed for flights > 6 hours |
| Local Transport | `local_transport` | Metro, bus, auto-rickshaw |
| Conference / Seminar Fee | `conference` | Must have prior manager approval |
| Visa & Travel Documents | `visa` | Actual cost, receipt required |
| Travel Insurance | `travel_insurance` | Company-approved plans only |

---

## 3. Ineligible Expenses (Non-Reimbursable)

The following expenses will be **rejected** regardless of receipt availability:

- **Minibar charges** (`minibar`) — personal consumption
- **Personal entertainment** (`entertainment`) — movies, personal outings
- **Gym / Spa** (`gym`, `spa`) — unless doctor-prescribed
- **Laundry** (`laundry`) — except for trips exceeding 5 consecutive days
- **Alcohol** (`alcohol`) — unless client entertainment (requires prior approval)
- **Traffic fines / Penalties** (`fine`) — not reimbursable
- **Luxury vehicle upgrades** — only standard taxi/cab class
- **Spouse or family travel** — not covered
- **Personal phone bills** — only business roaming charges with itemized bill

---

## 4. Per-Diem Limits

Refer to the **per_diem_limits.csv** file for city-wise daily limits.

**General rules:**
- Hotel limits are **per night**
- Meal limits are **per day** (breakfast + lunch + dinner combined)
- Taxi limits are **per day** for local conveyance
- Flight limits are **per trip** (one-way)

**If the city is not listed:** Use the nearest metro city limits and flag the claim for manual review.

**Exceeding limits:** Amounts above the per-diem cap will be deducted. The employee bears the excess cost.

---

## 5. Receipt Requirements

| Expense Amount | Receipt Required |
|---|---|
| Up to ₹200 | Not required |
| ₹201 – ₹500 | Digital/scanned receipt acceptable |
| Above ₹500 | Original receipt mandatory |
| Hotel bills | Must show itemized breakdown (room rate separate from F&B) |
| All flights | E-ticket / boarding pass required |

**Missing receipts:**
- Amounts ≤ ₹200: Approved without receipt
- Amount ₹201–₹500 without receipt: Reimbursed at **50%**
- Amount > ₹500 without receipt: **Rejected** unless emergency (requires manager email approval within 5 days)

---

## 6. Approval Matrix

Refer to **approval_matrix.csv** for employee grade-wise approval thresholds.

**General rules:**
- Claims within self-approval limit: Auto-approved (this agent handles this)
- Claims above manager approval limit: Escalated to Finance team
- Claims above Finance limit: Escalated to CFO

---

## 7. Booking Rules

### Flights
- Economy class for all domestic and international flights
- Business class allowed: flights > 6 hours OR medical reason with documentation
- Advance booking required: at least **48 hours** before travel
- Cancellation/change fees reimbursed only if business-reason cancellation

### Hotels
- **Domestic:** Maximum 4-star rated hotels
- **International:** Maximum 5-star rated hotels (Director-level approval needed)
- Airbnb/serviced apartments allowed with prior HR approval
- No hotel reimbursements for hometown/base city travel

### Meals
- Self-meal: Standard per-diem applies
- Client entertainment meals: Up to **2x per-diem**, requires guest list and business purpose

---

## 8. Special Cases & Exceptions

### Emergency Expenses
If receipt is unavailable due to emergency:
- Manager email approval required within **5 business days**
- Maximum reimbursable without receipt: ₹2,000 per trip

### International Travel
- Refer to `international_policy.md` for enhanced limits and forex rules
- All foreign currency expenses to be converted at **RBI reference rate on travel date**
- Forex receipts required for amounts > $50 equivalent

### First & Last Day of Travel
- Meal per-diem at **75%** for partial travel days

---

## 9. Submission Process

1. Submit claim via the reimbursement portal within 30 days
2. Attach all receipts (scanned PDFs or clear photos)
3. Claim reviewed by AI agent for policy compliance
4. Auto-approved or escalated based on approval matrix
5. Finance processes approved claims within **5 business days**

---

## 10. Policy Violations

Submitting false or inflated claims is a disciplinary offense and may result in:
- Recovery of incorrectly paid amounts
- Formal disciplinary action
- Termination in severe cases
